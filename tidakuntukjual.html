Tidak Untuk Jual

Depicting the reality of human trafficking through art in Indonesia

Skills

Interviewing
Cultural Immersion
Water Color

Problem and Opportunity

Increase public awareness of human trafficking through art
  • Human trafficking is a vast complicated issue that goes unnoticed by the general population
  • Art can be used as a technique to increase public awareness of the issue

My Work

Worked with local artists and NGOs in Bali, Indonesia to gain a deeper understanding of human trafficking and art for social change
  • Spoke with experts to understand the role of law enforcement and NGOs
  • Spoke with locals about their awareness of human trafficking around them
  • Researched the impact of globalization
  • Immersed myself in the local art community to understand the potential impact of art for social change
  • Created an art for social change piece for the Institute Seni Indonesia art exhibition to raise awareness

Results and Future Work

Art display at the Institute Seni Indonesia successfully raised questions among viewers
