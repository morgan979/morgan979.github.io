Cognitive Bias Training Module

How to collect unbiased data from user testing

Skills

User Testing
Cognitive Bias
Study Design
Presenting

Problem and Opportunity

Preventable medical errors in hospitals remain unacceptably due to lack of usability of medical devices
  • A simple training module has the ability to show people how to properly test medical devices that will be safe and intuitive to use in the field

My Work

Researched, filmed, and designed a training module
  • In depth research into different types of cognitive bias
  • Filmed examples comparing effective and ineffective ways to run a study
  • Created a Keynote presentation to succinctly and clearly deliver information

Results

Presented training module to clients
  • Presented training module to clients before testing a post-knee replacement rehabilitation device in order to reduce cognitive bias in the study
  • When presented at the HFES Medical Device Symposium it resulted in obtaining Johnson & Johnson as a client
Running the study for the post-knee replacement rehabilitation device
Filming videos for the training module
Training module on preventing cognitive bias during usability studies
