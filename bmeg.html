BMEG

An app that eases the process of choosing environmentally friendly buildings post disaster relief

Skills

Ionic
Agile Research
HMTL

Problem and Opportunity

Construction professionals struggle to choose environmentally friendly and cost effective buildings post natural disasters
  • Building an app will streamline the process of choosing which materials to use based on the location of the natural disaster
  • The app aims to encourage more environmentally friendly building choices

Our Work

Conducted need finding and app design
  • Conducted need finding among experts in the field including those most familiar with the construction professionals' needs and the building materials
  • Designed the task flow that best fits user needs
  • Connected app to algorithm, currently under development, that chooses best building option based on available materials
  • Developed an app in Ionic that allows users to specify information about their region, select if any materials are unavailable, and output the cheapest and most environmentally friendly building options

Results

An app that works within the limitations of the algorithm and that can easily be modified to encompass more circumstances
  • WWF (World Wide Fund) and Northwestern clients are looking forward to deploying the app
  • The current algorithm only works for a select few materials so future iterations of the app will take into account all combinations of available materials
